NatPK Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

This website will contain a blog of a person's experience visiting National Parks. It will also contain information such as wildlife and plants found in specific parks. Lastly, it will contain a gallery of pictures from various parks.

Target Audience

People who are interested in visiting National Parks or need information regarding them.

Typography

I chose a serif font (Cinzel) for the headings and sans-serif (Dosis) for the body. The serif font I chose looks professional and contrasts nicely with the body font.
